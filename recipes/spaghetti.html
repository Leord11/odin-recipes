Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Hotdogs
  • Ground meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato paste

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta Al dente
  2. Cook pasta sauce
  3. Cook the hotdogs first, then set aside
  4. Cook the ground meat , add some salt and pepper
  5. Add the hotdogs in the ground meat
  6. Add the Tomato Sauce and let it cook for a couple of minutes
  7. Add tomato paste with pasta water
  8. Let it cook for 10 minutes and voila
